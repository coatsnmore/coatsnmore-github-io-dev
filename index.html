How to Play

  1. Shoot bad guys for points.
  2. Don't fly into them.
  3. Don't get hit by their laser bubbles.
  4. Don't go out of bounds.
  5. Collect the rings!

Controls

  1. Up = Up
  2. Down = Down
  3. Left = Left
  4. Right = Right
  5. Space = Fire

Caveat
